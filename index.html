Pong, groundbreaking electronic game released in 1972 by the American game manufacturer Atari, Inc. One of the earliest video games, Pong became wildly popular and helped launch the video game industry.

My project here recreates the two-player game with a brand new design, convenient mechanics and some in-game boosts (some of them are still in progress). It is also available to enter players' names and see current scores.

The first solid-state electronic calculator was created in the early 1960s. Pocket-sized devices became available in the 1970s, especially after the Intel 4004, the first microprocessor, was developed by Intel for the Japanese calculator company Busicom.

In my project I set myself the task of building my own calculator app that will perform basic arithmetic operations. The project was realised using polish notation functions and some CSS designs.
